About Olympus Gym
YOUR TEMPLE

Olympus Gym is Sangli's highest-rated fitness center, earning a perfect 5.0 rating from over 90 satisfied members. Located in Vishrambag, near Mount Sinai English School, we offer a complete fitness experience designed for every level.

Under the expert guidance of Sanjay Gaikwad Sir, members receive personalized attention, structured diet advice, and workout programs that deliver real, measurable results. Whether you're a beginner or a competitive athlete — Olympus is your arena.

From powerlifting to functional fitness, from weight loss to muscle gain — our state-of-the-art facility and dedicated trainers will guide you every step of the way.
